# Privacy Policy for GravityShot

**Effective Date:** February 25, 2026
**Last Updated:** February 25, 2026

GameDo Studio ("we", "our", "us") built GravityShot as a free, ad-supported game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

## Information We Collect

### Information We Do NOT Collect

GravityShot does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not require account creation, login, or registration. All game progress (level completion, unlocked skins, settings) is stored **locally on your device only** and is never sent to any server.

### Information Collected by Third-Party Services

GravityShot uses **Google AdMob** to display rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

- Advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android)
- Device information (model, operating system version, screen size)
- IP address (used for approximate location, not stored precisely)
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## How Ads Work in GravityShot

GravityShot uses **rewarded video ads only**. Ads are never shown automatically or forced on the user. You may choose to watch a short video ad to unlock certain cosmetic skins. This is entirely optional and does not affect core gameplay.

## Data Storage

All game data is stored locally on your device using Godot Engine's built-in file storage. This includes:

- Level completion progress
- Unlocked skins
- Collected gravity cores
- Audio and display settings

This data is never transmitted off your device. Uninstalling the app will delete all saved data.

## Children's Privacy

GravityShot does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we do not collect any personal data ourselves, no special provisions are needed. However, Google AdMob may serve ads according to its own policies regarding children's content.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has been exposed to inappropriate content through ads, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

## Your Choices

- **Ad tracking:** You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:
  - **iOS:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  - **Android:** Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **Delete data:** You can delete all game data by uninstalling the app.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
